Frequently Asked Questions

What is the date and time of the reunion?

The Apache Junction High School Class of 2015 10-Year Reunion is scheduled for October 11, 2025, from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM.

Where will the reunion be held?

The location is currently TBD. We’ll update all registered guests as soon as the venue is confirmed.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is Black & Gold Semi-Formal. We encourage you to embrace the theme with your attire!

How much is the entry fee?

The entry fee is $50 per guest, which includes access to the event, food, and entertainment.

Can I bring a guest?

Yes, you may bring one guest. Please note this is a 21+ event, and children are not permitted.

How do I RSVP for the reunion?

You can RSVP by clicking the “RSVP” link in the navigation bar or directly via this link.

Who can I contact for more information?

For any questions or additional information, please contact our reunion committee at ajhs2015reunion@gmail.com.
